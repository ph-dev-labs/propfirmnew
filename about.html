A few years ago, a small team of people determined to transform banking launched a savings app for everyone. That app was the first step toward Prop firm Bank. Today, we’re even more determined and we’ve built a Central Bank-licensed, microfinance bank to help you get the best out of your money without overcharging you. Prop firm includes tools for tracking your spending habits, saving more and making the right money moves. So no matter who you are or where you live, we’re here because of you. We know the pain that comes with using a regular bank and we will make things work better for everyone

We envision to widen our customer reach to people of different races, countries and continents.
